About me:

I am a Ph.D. candidate in economics at Stanford University. I completed a Bachelor of Arts in mathematics and economics at the University of Chicago in 2015. From 2015 to 2017 I worked at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York as a research analyst in the Macroeconomic and Monetary Studies division of the Research group. My research focuses on macroeconomics and specifically on monetary economics and labor macroeconomics.
